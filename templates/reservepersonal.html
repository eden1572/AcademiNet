Appeals

    Test appeals is a procedure where students can submit a request for a re-examination of a test score or submitted work. Students can apply an appeal when they feel their test was not check correctly, that there was a misunderstanding or they wasn't giving enough time to complete the assignment. According to the regulations of the specific academy or institution, students can file an appeal within a fixed time after receiving the grade or feedback. During the appeal process, a student can submit a request document detailing the reasons for the given grade or for a re-examination. The appeal will then go through an internal process in the institution that will decide if to approve the correction or to disapprove it.
